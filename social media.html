Project Overview

This social media application is a fully-featured platform that allows users to connect, share moments, and communicate in real-time. Built with a React Native frontend and Python backend, the app delivers a seamless experience across iOS and Android devices with features including messaging, stories, live streaming, and content discovery.

Key Features

Real-time Messaging

Instant messaging with read receipts, typing indicators, and multimedia support for seamless communication.

Stories

Share ephemeral content that disappears after 24 hours with interactive viewer metrics.

Live Streaming

Broadcast live video to followers with real-time comments and reactions.

Social Discovery

Find and connect with friends through intelligent recommendations and contact syncing.

Smart Notifications

Personalized alerts for relevant activities with granular control over preferences.

Privacy Controls

Comprehensive privacy settings to control visibility and data sharing preferences.
